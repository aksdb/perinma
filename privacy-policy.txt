Perinma is a desktop application developed by Andreas Schneider (support -at- aksdb.de).

All information it stores is stored locally on the user's device. It does not transmit
any data that is not actively configured and therefore implicitly approved by the user.
All data is transmitted only between the application and the configured provider (Google
and/or any number of CalDAV/CardDAV servers). No data is transmitted to any other
parties or to the author themself. The data transmitted is also confined to the data
necessary to fulfill the purpose of the application and provide its core functionality.
